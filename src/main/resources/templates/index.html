Personal station

Bring your own Tempest token

WxCenter can read directly from your Tempest WeatherFlow station once you supply a personal API token.

How the token works

Your token is saved locally in the browser tab — it's never sent anywhere except directly to the Tempest API to pull your station's data.

  • Generate a personal access token from your Tempest account
  • Paste it in once — WxCenter keeps it for the session
  • Station readings populate automatically alongside alerts and outlooks
